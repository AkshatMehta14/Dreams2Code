About Us

Our Mission

Dreams2Code is a non-profit organization that provides free coding classes to high school students in the Central NJ area. We have the vision of transforming our passion of computer science into education for underserved communities. Our goal is to provide students with the tools and resources to succeed in the digital age, and to inspire them to pursue careers in technology. Together, we aspire to spread this passion for technology through hackathons, classes, and aiding businesses. Dreams2Code strives to empower communities and foster a more inclusive future.
